***Banwari Lal Jindal Suiwala College Tosham (Haryana)***

WHAT WE STAND FOR
To provide Quality Higher education to the young boys and girls of this highly backward & rural region to meet the demand of industry, state and society through excellence in art, science, commerce & vocational education: to remain as a source of pride to Haryana & to the country.

We Are Providing an Environment for Our Students to Grow & Excel
The great visionary and philanthropist, Seth Banwari Lal Jindal’s inspiration and active support became fruitful to take the shape of Banwari Lal Suiwala College, Tosham in the year 1995. It was his vision and mission to provide quality higher education to the young boys and girls of this highly backward and rural region to meet the demands of industry, state and society through excellence in Arts, Commerce, Science, Computer and Vocational Education. The foundation of the college building was laid on December 27, 1992, and the first session of the College commenced on August 10, 1995. The college is being run by Shri Banwari Lal Jindal Educational Trust in this rural backward area of Tosham.

ABOUT COLLEGE
BLJS College Tosham is one of the premier colleges of the state of Haryana. The college was established by The BLJS Educational Trust in 1995 with the vision to impart Quality Higher Education to the students especially girls of this rural backward area. The main objective of the college is to contribute to the transformation of prevailing social and economic conditions so that values enshrined in the constitution of India: social justice, equality of opportunity, democratic freedom to all, tolerance and respect to all religions etc. may be brought closer to realization, with special emphasis on Haryana.
